Schedule Mode

Schedule mode determines which action buttons are available in the notification window before your device hibernates.

  • Strict Mode – No optional buttons. Your device will hibernate automatically based on the schedule.
  • Moderate Mode – You can snooze hibernation, but you cannot cancel it.
  • Flexible Mode – You can either snooze or cancel hibernation.
Snoozing hibernation extends the schedule by 5 minutes only. Hibernation will occur automatically after 3 snoozes.

Enabling / Disabling Schedule

You can enable or disable a schedule by clicking the checkbox on the schedule card.

Disabling a schedule pauses its hibernation task until it is enabled again, as long as the scheduled time period has not already passed.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between a boot schedule and a hibernate schedule? ⌄

A boot schedule defines when you are allowed to use your device. Outside of this time window, the device will not remain active.

A hibernate schedule determines when your device will automatically hibernate (turn off) based on the configured schedule.

Can I set how long my device hibernates? ⌄

You control how long your device remains hibernated by setting a boot schedule for that day. Once configured, you can choose Hibernate Now to apply it immediately.

What happens if I don’t want my device to hibernate? ⌄

Depending on the selected schedule mode, you may be able to snooze or cancel the hibernation. In Strict Mode, no action is available and hibernation will proceed automatically.
